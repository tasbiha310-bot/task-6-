## Dataset
CoreTech Cleaned Client Dataset


# EDA Results


## Highest Demand Service

The service with highest client demand is Web Development.
It shows that many clients require website-related solutions.


## City With Most Clients

Lahore has the highest number of clients.
This indicates stronger business presence in Lahore.


## Best Lead Source

Referral is one of the strongest lead sources.
Clients gained through referrals show good trust.


## Average Project Budget

The average project budget is approximately calculated from all projects.
This helps estimate expected project revenue.


## Highest Budget Service

Cloud Computing has the highest average project budget.
It generates higher-value projects compared to other services.


## Project Status Counts

Most projects are completed while some are active.
This shows good project delivery performance.


## Average Client Rating

The average client rating is high.
It indicates good customer satisfaction.


# Insights

1. Cloud Computing projects have higher budgets compared to other services.

2. Clients mostly come from online sources and referrals.

3. Lahore and Karachi have strong client presence.

4. Most projects are completed successfully.

5. Client ratings show positive satisfaction.


# Business Recommendations

1. Increase marketing for high-value services like Cloud Computing and AI Solutions.

2. Focus on referral programs because they bring quality clients.

3. Improve services in cities with fewer clients to expand business reach.
